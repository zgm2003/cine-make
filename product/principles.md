# Principles

1. Story first, image second, video last.
2. Codex owns pre-production assets, not final video rendering.
3. Every shot needs visible action and continuity anchors.
4. Still-image prompts must not pretend to be motion prompts.
5. Long stories are preserved and split into episodes, not crushed into one teaser.
6. External video-model packs use one visible action per task with start/end frames.
7. If identity is unstable, generate references before control frames.
8. A pretty random shot is a failed shot.
