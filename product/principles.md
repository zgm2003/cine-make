# Principles

1. Story first, image second, video last.
2. Codex owns pre-production assets, not final video rendering.
3. Every shot needs visible action and continuity anchors.
4. Still-image prompts must not pretend to be motion prompts.
5. External video-model packs must be segmented when platform limits require it.
6. If identity is unstable, generate references before storyboards.
7. A pretty random shot is a failed shot.
