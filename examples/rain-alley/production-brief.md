# Cine Make production brief

Turn a short suspense prose beat into a 30-second vertical noir short.

## North star

The viewer should feel that the girl notices the umbrella figure before the story explains anything. Suspense comes from restraint: rain, breath, neon reflection, and a delayed reveal.

## Hard boundary

This run prepares director assets, still-image storyboard prompts, and external video-tool prompts. It does not contain a rendered video.
