# Prompt pack

## Director rewrite

Rewrite the source into visible beats: stop, listen, look back, notice umbrella, decide whether to move.

## Shotlist

Create eight shots. Every shot needs visible action, micro-performance, shot size, camera movement, composition, lighting, still-image prompt, and continuity bridge.

## Storyboard

Every storyboard prompt is a still keyframe. Keep the girl in a black raincoat, wet short hair, and cold neon reflections.

## External video pack

Segment for external synthesis. Reference the keyframes and describe motion, not new story.
